Welcome

The project you are viewing is called the "Launch Cycle" project. In it, you are tasked with finding a topic interesting to you and researching it. Eventually, you become researched on the topic enough to create a product of learning based on your research. This product of learning combines both your researched topic and your web design skills learned throughout the course. The finished project is a webpage that displays your product of learning after researching a topic. We dedicated 20% of our time in class to our Launch Cycle project. The project is completed at the end of the semester and is graded as your final exam. To hasten the speed and efficiency of the website construction, we use a framework known as Bootstrap. Bootstrap is a library of code in which you can utilize a large amount of code by typing a smaller amount. Effectively reducing the time it takes to code certain aspects of a webpage. We used bootstrap to display our project.
